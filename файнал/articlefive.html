The development of medicine with the advent of the COVID-19 vaccine and the huge contribution of women in its creation.

by Shubay Nurgyla | 8 February 2024

With the advent of COVID-19 vaccines, medicine has risen to a new level. Scientific geniuses from all over the world have come together to develop and distribute vaccines that can cope with a global problem like covid. Women have made a huge contribution, demonstrating the success achieved on the path to gender equality in science and medicine.

In 2020, a disease spread to our land, after which scientists gathered to study and fight SARS-CoV-2. Scientists, using mRNA technologies, used revolutionary methods, deciphered its genetic composition, which was the key to developing a vaccine. mRNA-based vaccines cause cells to produce a protein of the virus, causing an immune response. Usually, it takes years to develop a vaccine, but it was achieved in just a few months, without compromising safety.

List of specialists who participated in the creation of the vaccine: Sarah Gilbert, Kathrin Jansen, Kunsulu Zakaria, Kizzmekia Corbett, Katalin Kariko, Elena Smolyarchuk, Sumati K, Lisa Jackson, Hanneke Schuitmaker, Dr. Ozlem Tureci.
