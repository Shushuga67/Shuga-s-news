II women's volleyball game among the CIS countries.

by Nuraga Shubert | August 13 2023

Closer to sports news, we move on to the recent final, II women's volleyball games among the countries of the friendly independent states. In total, five teams participated - Kazakhstan, Belarus, Tajikistan, Armenia and Russia. On August 13, 2023, in the cultural and sports complex "Chizhovka-Arena" Belarus, in the city of Minsk, the games between Kazakhstan and Armenia took place. The competition was serious and the game tense. But our beautiful girls from the national team were also well prepared both physically and mentally. Kazakhstan has been one of the strongest teams in Central Asia and South Asia as a whole for years. Team of the Republic of Kazakhstan, No. 1 Volga Violetta, No. 2 Zhanabaeva Sagynysh, No. 3 Koilybaeva Balaim, No. 5, Serik Rabia, No. 6. Nikishkina Irina, No. 7 Kutova Maya, No. 8 Balmukhambetova Karina, No. 9 Aitugan Nargiz, No. 11 Tiyanakova Aikhanym, No. 16 Amerzhan Ayaulym. Head coach - Ergazin Rinad.

The first serve was from Kazakhstan, the game was very intriguing and exciting. The RK team defeated its opponents at the beginning of the first set. The girls showed their incredible attacks, techniques, blocking, defence and endurance, which decided the outcome of the game. But the opponents did not lag behind and the first set favoured Armenia, with a score of 24/26. In the second set, our team also played an amazing game, annoying the opponents with its speed and attack power. Thanks to this, she managed to snatch victory with the last and brilliant serve from the number one player, Volga Violetta. The score of the second set is 25/15 in favour of Kazakhstan. The coherence of our team could not be ignored; everyone fought until the last drop of sweat. Thus our team won again in the third set, with a score of 25/11. Kazakhstan did not stand still and decided to bring us victory in the fourth set with a score of 25/10. In general, the result was 3:1 in our favour. Armenia fought to the last and was a worthy opponent.

We are very proud of our girls who brought a bronze medal from the Commonwealth Games 2023. And we hope that they will continue to bring us victories and also gold!
